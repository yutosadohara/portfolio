About onsen

Hime no Yu offers a large bathtub and private baths. Students of the International College of Technology can use the baths free of charge. Closed every Thursday. Numerous students use this hot spring.

Use by the general public

In the large bathtub, adults can use it for 500 yen, children can use it for 350 yen, and infants can use it for free. Private baths are available for 45 minutes at a time. Extensions are available for 300 yen per 15 minutes.

About the large bathtub

Learn more about large bathtubs. The large bathtub is separated for men and women. Therefore, there are two large bathtubs, and the large bathtub is changed on a daily basis. In the large bathtubs, there are saunas, pressure point baths, box steam baths, and various other types of hot springs. Himenoyu uses hot spring water, so it feels better than ordinary home baths.
